FAQ

Common
questions.

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out directly — we usually respond within a few hours.

How long does a typical website project take?
+
Most websites take 2–4 weeks from kickoff to launch. Complex web apps or e-commerce projects with custom features can take 6–10 weeks. We always give you a clear timeline upfront in the proposal.
Do you work with international clients?
+
Yes, absolutely. While we're based in Mumbai, we serve clients across India, the UAE, UK, USA, and Australia. All communication and project management happens seamlessly online.
What do I need to get started?
+
Just book a free discovery call. We'll ask you about your business, goals, and what you're looking for. From there, we handle the rest — design, development, content suggestions, and launch.
Can I update the website myself after launch?
+
Yes. We build with user-friendly CMS options so you can make content updates easily. We also provide a training session post-launch so you're comfortable managing your site independently.
Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?
+
Yes, all packages include a support period. After that, we offer monthly retainer plans that cover updates, performance monitoring, security patches, and ongoing growth work.
